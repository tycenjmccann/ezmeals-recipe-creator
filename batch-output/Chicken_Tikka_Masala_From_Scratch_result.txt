## 🔑 Key Chef Review Changes (20 total)

| Category | Changes |
|----------|---------|
| **Critical Protein Fix** | **Switched from chicken breast → chicken thighs** — all 3 reference recipes (BBC Good Food, Cafe Delites, RecipeTin Eats) prefer/insist on thighs. Breasts dry out during high-heat charring and extended simmering. |
| **Critical Spice Fix** | **Reduced cayenne from 2 tsp → 1/4 tsp** — 2 tsp is aggressively hot (~20,000+ SHU contribution). RecipeTin uses 1/8 tsp, Cafe Delites uses 1 tsp mild Kashmiri chili. Added scaling note. |
| **Missing Spices Added** | **Turmeric** (1/2 tsp marinade + 1 tsp sauce) — signature CTM color/flavor, in all comparisons but missing from original. **Ground coriander** (1 tsp each) — standard tikka masala spice across all references. |
| **Sauce Fixes** | Doubled tomato sauce 8→14 oz + 1/2 cup water (8 oz was too thin); increased butter 1→2 tbsp + 2 tbsp finishing butter (richness defines restaurant CTM); increased oil 1→2 tbsp; added 1 tsp sugar to balance acidity |
| **Technique** | Added **batch cooking** instruction (prevents steaming vs. charring); added **spice blooming step** (30-60 sec in fat); added **sauce blending step** (optional, for restaurant silkiness); added oil to marinade; added ginger to sauce (was missing) |
| **Accuracy/Safety** | Fixed paprika omission from Step 1; corrected cook time 35→45 min; added 165°F internal temp requirement; added "cook in batches" + visual cues; split cilantro (half cooking, half garnish); reduced cinnamon 1→1/2 tsp |
| **Completeness** | Added make-ahead/storage/reheating notes; added dairy-free substitution; added oil choice guidance; clarified marinating time in total |
