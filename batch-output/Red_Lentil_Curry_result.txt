## 🔑 Key Chef Review Changes (18 total)

| Category | Changes |
|----------|---------|
| **Critical Missing Spice** | **Added 1/2 tsp ground turmeric** — near-universal in dal/lentil curries for earthy backbone and golden color. Its absence was the recipe's single biggest gap. |
| **Under-Spicing Fixed** | Increased cumin 1/2→1 tsp; increased curry powder 1/2→1 tsp (original was conservative for the volume — most comparable recipes use 1-1.5 tsp of primary spices) |
| **Garam Masala Timing** | **Moved from spice bloom (Step 3) to finishing (Step 6) off heat** — garam masala is a finishing spice blend with volatile aromatics (cardamom, clove, cinnamon) that 20+ minutes of simmering destroys. This is the correct technique per all reference recipes. |
| **Seasoning & Balance** | Increased salt 1/2→3/4 tsp; increased lemon juice 1→1 1/2 Tbsp (rich curry needs more acid); added optional sugar/maple syrup to balance tomato acidity |
| **Technique** | Added "partially cover" during lentil simmer (prevents evaporation/scorching); added "skim foam" instruction (red lentils produce significant foam); added deglaze tip for spice blooming; added "shake coconut milk can" instruction; increased oil 1→1 1/2 Tbsp for proper blooming |
| **Authentic Elevation** | **Added optional tadka (tempering) step** — cumin seeds + sliced garlic + red pepper flakes sizzled in oil and poured over the finished curry. Bridges "weeknight easy" to "restaurant quality" with minimal effort. |
| **Completeness** | Added spinach/kale suggestion; added reheating guidance (curry thickens dramatically when cold); organized ingredients by cooking phase; added gluten-free notation with broth/curry powder caveat; added lemon wedges for serving |
