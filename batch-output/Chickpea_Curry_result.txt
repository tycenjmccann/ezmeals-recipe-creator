## 🔑 Key Chef Review Changes (15 total)

| Category | Changes |
|----------|---------|
| **Critical Technique Fix** | **Moved whole spice tempering (cumin + mustard seeds) to Step 1 BEFORE onions** — original had them AFTER garlic/ginger (Step 2). Whole spices must bloom in hot oil first (tadka/chaunk technique); adding after garlic risks burning garlic while seeds are still raw. Verified against Minimalist Baker and Budget Bytes. |
| **Critical Technique Fix** | **Moved garam masala to finish OFF HEAT** (Step 6) — was added with tomatoes in Step 3 and simmered 30 min. Garam masala is a finishing spice with volatile aromatics (cardamom, clove, cinnamon) that prolonged cooking destroys. Both Serious Eats and Minimalist Baker confirm off-heat addition. |
| **Technique** | Added ground spice toasting step (coriander + turmeric toasted 30-45 sec in oil before liquid); changed vessel from "large skillet" → "large pot or Dutch oven" (~10 cups of curry overflows a skillet); added explicit "reduce heat to medium-low" for simmer (was unspecified — would scorch) |
| **Seasoning** | Increased salt 1/2→3/4 tsp; added optional 1 tsp sugar to balance acidity from 2 cans tomatoes + paste + lime; reduced cilantro 1.5→1 cup and split into 1/2 cup cooking + 1/2 cup garnish (cooking builds herbal depth) |
| **Timing** | Corrected prep time 5→15 min (honest: dice onion + mince garlic/ginger + chop cilantro + drain chickpeas + juice lime); corrected cook time 45→50 min (2 min bloom + 15 min onion + 4 min aromatics + 5 min sauce + 25 min simmer = ~51 min); reduced simmer 30→20-25 min (proper spice blooming means shorter simmer; 30 min risks coconut milk separation) |
| **Safety & UX** | Added mustard seed splatter warning; added visual/tactile cues throughout ("shimmering" oil, "deep golden brown" onions, "coats back of a spoon"); added storage/leftover notes; added spinach/kale addition tip |
